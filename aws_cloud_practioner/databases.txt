ELASTICACHE
    ELASTICACHE IS A WEB SERVICE THAT MAKES IT EASY TO DEPLOY, OPERATE, AND SCALE AN IN-MEMORY CACHE IN THE CLOUD.
    THE SERVICE IMPORVES THE PERFORMANCE OF WEB APPLCICATIONS BY ALLOWING YOU TO RETRIEVE INFORMATION FROM FAST, MANAGED, IN-MEMORY CACHES,
        INSTEAD OF RELYING ENTIRELY ON SLOWER DISK-BASED DATABASES.

    ELASTICACHE SUPPORTS TWO OPEN-SOURCE IN-MEMORY CACHING ENGINES:
        - MEMCACHED
        - REDIS

    CACHING ENGINE IN THE CLOUD WITH MOST COMMON QUERIES.
